Typing Race Game Instructions

  1. Joining the Game Room: Join the game room where the typing race will take place. Wait for all players to join.
  2. Readying Up: Before the game starts, all players must ready up by typing "ready" to play. Once everyone has readied up, the game will begin.
  3. Countdown: A 5-second countdown will start once all players have readied up. Use this time to prepare for the upcoming round.
  4. Typing Challenge: After the countdown, a sentence will be displayed for you to type. Your goal is to type the sentence correctly, as quickly as you can.
  5. Winning the Round: The player who correctly types the sentence first wins the round. Typing speed and accuracy are crucial in determining the winner.
  6. Scoring: Each round won earns the player a point. Points are tallied throughout the game.
  7. Round Limit: For this game, the players can play as many rounds as they want to.
  8. Determining the Winner: The player with the most points at the end of all rounds wins the game.
